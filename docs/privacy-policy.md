# Privacy Policy for Perched

Effective date: February 11, 2026

Perched ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use the Perched mobile application and related services.

This policy is intended to comply with Apple App Store Review Guideline 5.1.1.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Location Data
- Approximate and precise location data is used for spot discovery, map relevance, and check-in verification.
- Location may be attached to check-ins when you explicitly post activity.

### 1.2 Account and Profile Data
- Email address
- Display name / username
- Profile photo (if provided)
- Optional campus/city profile details

### 1.3 Check-in and User-Generated Content
- Check-in metadata (spotPlaceId, spot name, timestamps)
- Captions and optional photos
- Workspace metrics ratings (WiFi, noise, busyness, outlets)
- Reactions, comments, and social interactions

### 1.4 Device and Usage Data
- Device and app version information
- Crash logs and diagnostic metadata
- Usage interactions (screens viewed, filters used, feature engagement)
- Performance metrics (latency/error telemetry)

## 2. How We Use Your Data

We process data to:
- Provide spot discovery and real-time workspace intelligence.
- Show friend activity and social features.
- Generate crowd-sourced spot metrics (WiFi, noise, busyness, outlets).
- Improve app reliability, monitor performance, and fix bugs.
- Support premium subscription features.
- Operate a B2B API using anonymized and aggregated data only (no direct PII shared).

## 3. Third-Party Services We Use

Perched integrates the following services:
- Firebase (Authentication, Firestore/Storage, analytics signals, crash/diagnostic pipelines)
- Foursquare API (place enrichment)
- Yelp API (place enrichment)
- OpenStreetMap (place enrichment)
- RevenueCat (subscription management)
- Sentry (error tracking)

Each provider may process data under its own terms and privacy policy.

## 4. Data Sharing and Visibility

- Public check-ins may be visible to all Perched users.
- Friends-only check-ins are visible only to approved friends.
- Friends can see your activity if your visibility settings allow it.
- Aggregated, anonymized insights may be shared with B2B partners.
- We do not sell personal information as an individual profile.

## 5. Data Retention

- Check-ins are retained until you delete them or delete your account.
- Session location context is used for real-time functionality and retained only as needed for product operation.
- Analytics/performance telemetry is retained for up to 90 days unless a shorter retention window is configured.

## 6. Your Rights and Controls

You can:
- Delete your account and associated data.
- Request export of your check-in history.
- Control check-in visibility (public vs friends-only).
- Opt out of non-essential analytics where available.

To submit a request, contact support@perched.app.

## 7. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect your data. No system is 100% secure.

## 8. Children

Perched is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us and we will investigate and remove data where appropriate.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material updates will be reflected by updating the effective date.
